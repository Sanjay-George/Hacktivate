  • Rules
  1. The Hacktivate is organised exclusively for the employees of the organisation, including interns.
  2. Each team can have a maximum of 3 members.
  3. Criteria for judging:
    • Quality of the project.
    • Justification of the project idea.
    • Utility of the idea with respect to the organisation.
    • The winners will be selected by a panel of judges.
  4. Contestants can pick a task from a list of problems provided, or come up with their own idea which solves the business problem in CarWale
  5. The submitted project will be the intellectual property of the organisation.
  6. All the participants should warrant that the submission made is not infringing any third party rights. The submission should not contain malware of any kind.
  7. The winning team will be awarded a cash prize of ₹25,000/-.
  8. Teams must stop hacktivating once the time is up.
  9. Teams should be aware of any third libraries used in the project.
  10. Event Day Schedule (24 hours):

    10AM - 11AM : IDEATHON

    11AM - 1 PM : PLANNING

    1 PM - 2 PM : LUNCH BREAK

    2PM onwards : HACKATHON
